About YTInsight

Built for creators who take growth seriously

YTInsight was built by The Digital Edge (TDE) — a Pakistan-based digital product studio — to solve a real problem: YouTube creators waste hours guessing which titles, tags, and thumbnails will perform.

We replaced guesswork with intelligence. YTInsight combines live YouTube data with Claude AI to give every creator an unfair advantage, regardless of channel size.

Whether you're a solo creator or managing a team of content producers, YTInsight's platform adapts to your workflow with a clean dashboard, persistent history, and collaboration tools built right in.

Documentation

Get up and running in minutes

YTInsight requires minimal setup. Here's everything you need to know.

Step 1
Create an Account
Click "Get Started" or "Login" and register with your email. Your account is secured via Firebase Authentication.
Step 2
Add Your YouTube API Key
Go to Dashboard → API Settings. Paste your YouTube Data API v3 key — it's stored locally in your browser, never on our servers.
Step 3
Analyze Any Channel or Video
Paste a YouTube channel handle, channel URL, or video URL into the input bar and click Analyze Now.
Step 4
Generate SEO & Thumbnails
Use the SEO Generator to create titles, descriptions, and tags. Analyze thumbnails for CTR coaching and AI-generated concepts.
